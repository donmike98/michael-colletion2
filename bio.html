Inspiration behind the collection

My inspiration behind collecting images of recent burger's I have ate started when I was 17 years old. It all began with a simple snapchat, then everytime I went to a resturant I started to save the pitures of the meal. This lead to my small collection building over the months. I wanted to keep my collection of images for further comparison from different well-known restuarants.

My current collection

From the images you can see my collection has 6 images and all from various popular restuarants. The resturants the burgers come from include; TGI Friday's, Tinsletown, Frankies and Bennies, Fatburger Diner, Burger and lobster plus finaly The Diner.

The future for my collection

In hope of creating this collection, I want to inspire others to take photos of there meal as I want them to see the craft behind making a burger and it's complexity. I would also like others to send there images and added into my collection.
